## 1. Information you should never enter into AI tools

Do not input the following into AI tools:

- [ ] Personal identifying information (full name, ID numbers, contact details, etc.)
- [ ] Other people's personal information

- [ ] Medical records

## 2. How to evaluate AI output

AI can be wrong. Follow these principles:

- **Verify**: Always confirm important information with official sources.
- **Think critically**: Do not accept AI responses without evaluation.
- **Consult professionals**: Seek expert advice for legal, medical, or financial decisions.
